Solar Wall Light

3 Lighting Modes

Mode 1: it is in constant 50% brightness mode whole night;
Mode 2: becomes 100% brightness when people come for 15 seconds and returns to 20% bright mode when the people leave;
Mode 3: light will automatically turn on at 100% high brightness when people come for 15 seconds, and turn off when the people leave.

Energy Saving & Long Working Time

Solar lights has built-in rechargeable battery ensures full charge at day time. You just need to place the solar panel under direct sunlight at day. No pollution, no electricity cost, energy saving and eco-friendly.
High power daylight 6000K LED with 270 degree beam angle. Provide maximum illumination for large spaces.
